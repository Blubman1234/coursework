What are CRT & SRT

CRT and SRT are both different forms of reaction time. CRT stands for Choice Reaction Time and is the amount of time it takes for your brain to process a choice and select an option. SRT stands for Simple Reaction Time and is the amount of time it takes for you to react to a simple stimulus.
